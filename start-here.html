“Start Here” Intake Session

A one-time orientation conversation to see whether the Voice Lab or Webpage-as-Healing-Tool process is the right fit for you right now.

The best place to begin if you’re interested in the Voice Lab or the Webpage-as-Healing-Tool process is the Start Here Intake Session, where we can explore whether this work is the right fit for you in this season of your life.

If you’re unsure where to begin, the Start Here intake session is a one-time conversation designed to orient you — gently, clearly, and without pressure.

In this session, we will:

  • Briefly map your trauma landscape without forcing details
  • Identify your most pressing concerns and patterns
  • Introduce basic nervous-system concepts relevant to your story
  • Clarify whether 1:1 support, group circles, or the webpage-as-healing-tool process is the best next step
  • Discuss scope and boundaries so you know exactly what I can and cannot offer

This session is about orientation, not obligation. You decide if and how you’d like to engage further.

How to Request a Start Here Session

To request a Start Here intake session, please use the contact form on the main page and mention “Start Here” in your message.
